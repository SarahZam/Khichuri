Bengali Khichuri Recipe

About This Dish

Khichuri in a South Asian comfort dish made with lentils, rice, and spices. Bengali Khichuri is cooked with seasonal vegetables and is usually consumed in winter or on rainy days. It's also fed to patients since the ingredients make it a very healthy meal. Sometimes, mothers make this dish for their kids whenever they have a bad day which leads to aforementioned kids making a website centered around this dish in their csc courses because they haven't been home for months thanks to the curse of 2020 and they just want their mom to make them some khichuri.
